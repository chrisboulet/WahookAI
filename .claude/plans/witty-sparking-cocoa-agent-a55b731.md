# Research Plan: B2B Marketing for Fractional CTO in Quebec City

## Objective
Research actionable B2B marketing strategies for a fractional CTO consultant targeting PME owners (10-100 employees) in Quebec City with 40-75K$/year contracts.

## Research Areas

### 1. LinkedIn Strategies for Quebec Fractional Executives
- Quebec-specific LinkedIn tactics for consultants
- Francophone business owner engagement patterns
- Content themes that resonate with PME decision-makers
- Success stories from Quebec fractional executives
- LinkedIn groups active in Quebec business community

### 2. Business Associations & Networking Groups
- Chambers of commerce in Quebec City
- PME-focused business associations
- Technology and innovation groups
- Industry-specific associations (manufacturing, services, etc.)
- Networking events calendars

### 3. Strategic Partnership Opportunities
- Accounting firms serving PMEs in Quebec City
- Business law firms with SMB practice
- Business coaches and consultants
- Management consulting firms
- Referral partnership models

### 4. Local Events & Conferences
- Annual business conferences in Quebec City
- Technology events and meetups
- Chamber of commerce events
- Industry trade shows
- Networking breakfast/lunch series

### 5. Content Marketing for Francophone Business Owners
- Language considerations (French vs bilingual)
- Topics that resonate with Quebec PME owners
- Formats preferred by francophone audiences
- Local case studies and examples
- Quebec-specific business challenges to address

## Research Method
Use multi-source parallel research (Standard mode - 3 agents per type):
- Claude WebSearch for free comprehensive coverage
- Multiple search angles for Quebec-specific insights
- Focus on 2024-2025 current information
